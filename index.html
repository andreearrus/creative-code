Real-time Drawing

Welcome to Real-time drawing application! To start, ensure your webcam is connected and you grant access to it when prompted. Once you see your video feed, point your index finger towards the camera. You can then "draw" in the space captured by your camera. The application will follow your index finger's movement, drawing lines on a canvas the video feed.

Choose your desired stroke color using the provided color picker and adjust the stroke width with the slider. The selected width is displayed right above the canvas for your reference. If you'd like to clear your drwaing and start anew, just say the word "clear" aloud or click on "Reset" button and the canvas will reset.
